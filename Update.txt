The core problem solved: A mid-year student like Arjun (joined December) has months June–November marked as N/A — they never appear as "due", never inflate your pending list, and never reduce your revenue figure. Your profit calculation only counts enrolled months.
Three fee statuses for enrolled months:

Paid — collected, counts in revenue
Partial — half paid (for cases where student paid part of the month)
Due — pending, shows in the pending tab

Pending fees tab only shows students who have actual dues from their joining month onward — not N/A months, so your list stays clean and accurate.
Revenue tab shows expected vs collected vs pending — expected is calculated only from enrolled months, so N/A months never distort your numbers.
